About Prof. Haitao ZHAO

Prof. Haitao ZHAO is a Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He serves as the Director of the Research Centre for Materials Intelligent Manufacturing (RCMIM) and is the Academic Leader of the State Key Laboratory of Ultra-precision Machining Technology.

His research integrates robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and data-driven methodologies to revolutionize material research and development. The goal is to revolutionize material R&D through Robotic Factory (Hardware) and AI Factory (Software).

Research Interests

Material Intelligence

Advanced research in intelligent materials and their applications in modern manufacturing and technology. Developing novel materials with enhanced properties through AI-driven discovery and optimization processes.

Machine Intelligence

Development of AI and machine learning algorithms for materials science and engineering applications. Creating intelligent systems that can predict material behaviors and optimize manufacturing processes.

Embodied Intelligence

Integration of robotics and AI to create intelligent systems for materials research and development. Building autonomous systems that can perform complex material characterization and processing tasks.
